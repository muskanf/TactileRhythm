# Tactile Rhythm

Tactile Rhythm is a simple web-based prototype that converts text-based drum rhythm specifications into accessible representations.

The MVP currently generates:

1. an accessible table,
2. a navigable text version,
3. a tactile-style SVG preview.

## Example Input

```text
title: Basic Rock Beat
time: 4/4

hihat: x x x x x x x x
snare: . . x . . . x .
kick:  x . . . x . . .
